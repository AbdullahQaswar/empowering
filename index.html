Our Curriculum for JAVA

Our Curriculem for JAVA is designed to be a beginner friendly course. Classes will be held from August 19th-30th only on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays from 10-12AM.

DAY ONE

Students will be introduced to the JAVA language and our curriculem. On day one they will learn the basics including Main, Print Statements, and Variables. They will also have time to practice.

DAY TWO

On Day two the students will continue on the basics with learning the different operators in JAVA, and Strings. As usual, they will have time to practice what they learn. We will also have a knowledge check at the end of day two.

DAY THREE

On day three the students will be diving into more complex parts of JAVA. Now that they know operators it will be time to take them into Logical Operators and if else statements.

DAY FOUR

On day four we will have the students tackle Loops including While Loops and For Loops. Loops are very complex so we will ensure that the students have enough time to practice them well.

DAY FIVE

On day five we will have the students learn the finishing touches of the language, including Arrays, Methods and Classes. We will also introduce the final project on day five.

DAY SIX

On day six we will go through the students final projects. With all of this the students will have an advanced understanding of the JAVA language and are able to code the language with above-average standards.
