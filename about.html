About EYIS

Non-Profit

Here at EYIS we strive for allowing the youth to be empowered in the new world heavily relying on software where there is a high likelyhood of their future career relating to computer science.

We aim to accomplish this while being approachable towards our students and want to teach them in a friendly and engaging way. With this we try to have our classes as educational and engaging as possible making each student feel at home.


Why Choose Us?

Here at EYIS we have created a well-structured program for our students to learn JAVA in the quickest and most efficient way possible with several experienced teachers.

On top of this, the course is free with it being short and sweet, it will barely take any of our students time. Meaning that it isn't a huge commitment, so there is quite literally nothing you can lose by signing up!
